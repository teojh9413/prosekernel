# Ahrefs homepage problem aware SEO promise

## Source
- Author: Ahrefs
- URL: https://ahrefs.com/
- Rights note: Metadata and original craft analysis only; do not mirror full source text.

## Why this is good
Ahrefs works as copy because it does not pretend the reader is unaware of SEO. It speaks to a problem-aware audience: they want more search traffic and need tools to do the work. The page can therefore prioritize outcomes, capabilities, and proof rather than category education.

## Craft moves
- Assumes meaningful reader awareness instead of over-explaining the category.
- Connects tool features to an outcome the buyer already values.
- Uses product breadth as credibility.
- Keeps the homepage pointed at a practical job.

## Structure map
1. Outcome promise.
2. Audience/job clarity.
3. Capability clusters.
4. Proof and conversion path.

## Excerpt or summary
Homepage positioning and structure analysis only; no page copy mirrored.

## Reusable pattern
For problem-aware buyers, skip generic education and show how the product helps them do the job faster or better.

## Imitation prompt
Write homepage copy for a problem-aware technical buyer: outcome, job, capability clusters, proof, next step.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Teaching basics to expert readers.
- Leading with internal feature taxonomy.
- Making a broad promise without showing the work supported.
