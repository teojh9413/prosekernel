## Why this is good
The launch video proves that humor works best when it carries the offer instead of decorating it. The viewer quickly understands what is sold, why the incumbent option is annoying, and what action to take. Personality reduces friction because the economics are already clear.

## Craft moves
- States the offer plainly before jokes can obscure it.
- Turns category frustration into a shared enemy.
- Uses founder presence as trust and entertainment.
- Keeps the call to action simple and low-cognitive-load.

## Structure map
1. Plain offer.
2. Enemy/problem in the existing category.
3. Fast proof through demonstration and contrast.
4. Simple action.

## Excerpt or summary
A founder-led commodity launch built on clear offer, price contrast, and comedic pacing; no transcript stored.

## Reusable pattern
If the category is boring, make the value unmistakable first, then let personality make it shareable.

## Imitation prompt
Write a launch script for a boring product: open with the offer, name the customer frustration, prove the value, and end with one action.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Letting jokes replace the proposition.
- Being quirky before being clear.
- Making the viewer infer price, audience, or next step.
