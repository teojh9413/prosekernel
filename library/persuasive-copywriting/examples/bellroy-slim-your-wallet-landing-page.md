# Bellroy slim your wallet landing page

- Author: Bellroy

## Why this is good
Bellroy’s wallet copy works because it translates a product advantage into a visible before/after. The page teaches that thinness is not a claim to repeat; it is a contrast to demonstrate. The writing supports the proof rather than competing with it.

## Craft moves
- Uses comparison as evidence.
- Makes the pain tactile and easy to picture.
- Connects design detail to daily annoyance.
- Keeps copy secondary to the demonstration.

## Structure map
1. Show the common pain.
2. Display the physical contrast.
3. Explain the design choices that produce the result.
4. Point to product options.

## Excerpt or summary
A landing-page example centered on wallet bulk, comparison, and visible benefit; analysis only.

## Reusable pattern
When the benefit is visual, make the page prove it before the copy argues it.

## Imitation prompt
Write landing-page copy for this product using a before/after proof structure: pain, visible contrast, mechanism, choice.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Using abstract adjectives for a physical benefit.
- Hiding the comparison below generic positioning.
- Explaining design without showing why it matters.
