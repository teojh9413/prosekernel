# Material Design content design overview

- Author: Google Material Design

## Why this is good
Material’s content design guidance is strong because it treats words as part of system design. It gives teams a shared language for clarity, hierarchy, action, and consistency instead of leaving microcopy to taste debates.

## Craft moves
- Positions content as product structure.
- Uses principles that scale across components.
- Connects clarity to user action.
- Creates consistency without making every surface sound identical.

## Structure map

## Reusable pattern
For design-system content, state principles that help teams decide what users need to know, do, and trust at each surface.

## Imitation prompt
Write UX content guidance that turns clarity, hierarchy, and action into reusable team decisions.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Treating microcopy as cosmetic polish.
- Writing principles too vague for component decisions.
- Pursuing consistency at the cost of context.
