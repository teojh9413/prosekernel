# Paul Graham Essay Insight to Social Compression

## Source
- Author: ProseKernel synthesis from Paul Graham-style essays
- URL: https://paulgraham.com/articles.html
- Rights note: Analysis only.

## Why this is good
The useful move is not to copy essay style into social media. It is to compress one hard-won distinction into a post that still feels intelligent.

## Craft moves
- Starts with a surprising distinction.
- Avoids motivational fluff.
- Uses simple words for abstract ideas.
- Leaves the reader with a reusable mental model.

## Structure map
1. Distinction.
2. Why people miss it.
3. Example.
4. Consequence.
5. Compressed rule.

## Excerpt or summary
Essay thinking becomes viral when the core distinction is sharp enough to travel.

## Reusable pattern
“The mistake is thinking X. The better distinction is Y vs Z.”

## Imitation prompt
Extract the central distinction from this essay idea and turn it into a 5-part social post: distinction, mistake, example, consequence, rule.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Turning deep thought into generic advice.
- Copying a writer’s tone without their reasoning.
