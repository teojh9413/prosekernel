# A16Z software is eating the world thesis

## Why this is good
The essay became culturally durable because its core frame is vivid, directional, and useful for interpreting many events. It shows how a thesis can be both analytically broad and linguistically compact.

## Craft moves
- Names a macro shift with an image-like phrase.
- Supports the frame across multiple sectors.
- Turns scattered examples into one explanatory lens.
- Makes the phrase usable by other writers.

## Structure map
1. Memorable thesis frame.
2. Historical/contextual setup.
3. Cross-category evidence.
4. Implications for readers and markets.

## Excerpt or summary
Market-thesis structure analysis only; no essay text mirrored.

## Reusable pattern
A strong thesis gives readers a reusable lens, then proves it across enough domains to feel inevitable.

## Imitation prompt
Write a market thesis with one memorable frame, then support it across three domains and explain the implication.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Using a catchy phrase without evidence.
- Listing examples without a unifying frame.
- Making the thesis so broad it cannot be falsified.
