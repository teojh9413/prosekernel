# Naval how to get rich tweetstorm

- Author: Naval Ravikant

## Why this is good
The tweetstorm spread because each unit is self-contained but also part of a larger thesis. It combines a provocative promise, compact principles, and enough sequence to feel like a curriculum rather than loose advice.

## Craft moves
- Starts with a high-desire outcome.
- Uses short principle statements that can stand alone.
- Sequences ideas from worldview to behavior.
- Balances compression with perceived depth.

## Structure map
1. Promise.
2. Principle series.
3. Contrasts and exclusions.
4. Operating rules.
5. Memorable close or continuation.

## Excerpt or summary
Metadata and structure analysis of a widely cited thread; no tweets mirrored.

## Reusable pattern
For social essays, make each unit independently quotable while preserving a cumulative argument.

## Imitation prompt
Turn this thesis into a social thread: start with a high-desire promise, then sequence standalone principles into a coherent argument.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Writing disconnected aphorisms.
- Making every post depend on previous context.
- Using a big promise without operational principles.
