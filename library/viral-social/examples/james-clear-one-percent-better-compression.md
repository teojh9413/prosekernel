## Why this is good
The one-percent-better idea travels because it compresses a behavior change philosophy into a tiny measurable unit. The writing makes improvement feel less heroic and more compoundable, which gives readers a handle they can repeat.

## Craft moves
- Uses a small number to defeat overwhelm.
- Connects daily behavior to compounding.
- Creates a phrase readers can reuse.
- Makes the abstract principle operational.

## Structure map
1. Tiny unit.
2. Compounding implication.
3. Contrast with dramatic change.
4. Practical application.

## Reusable pattern
For behavior-change writing, shrink the action until it feels doable, then show the compound effect.

## Imitation prompt
Write a social explainer that turns this intimidating goal into a small repeatable unit with compounding payoff.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Motivational intensity without a repeatable unit.
- Overstating precision from a metaphorical number.
- Skipping the reader’s felt overwhelm.
