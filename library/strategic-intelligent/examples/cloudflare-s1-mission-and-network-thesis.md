## Why this is good
Cloudflare’s S-1 material is a strong strategic source because it has to make technical infrastructure legible to capital markets without flattening the technical thesis. It connects mission, network effects, product expansion, and market category.

## Craft moves
- Explains the technical network as strategic advantage.
- Connects mission language to product and market expansion.
- Uses public filing constraints to force clarity.
- Makes infrastructure sound consequential without hype.

## Structure map
1. Mission and market context.
2. Network/product thesis.
3. Evidence of scale and customer need.
4. Expansion logic and risk-aware framing.
