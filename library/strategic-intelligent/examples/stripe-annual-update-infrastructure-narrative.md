# Stripe annual update infrastructure narrative

## Why this is good
Stripe annual updates work because they connect product progress to a long-running thesis about economic infrastructure. The writing gives readers specific proof while preserving the sense that individual launches belong to a broader system.

## Craft moves
- Frames company work inside a durable market thesis.
- Uses concrete product and adoption proof to avoid empty scale language.
- Connects operational details to strategic direction.
- Balances investor-style confidence with builder specificity.

## Structure map
1. State the broader shift.
2. Show progress through concrete proof.
3. Connect product areas to the thesis.
4. Point toward remaining work and opportunity.

## Reusable pattern
For annual updates, make each proof point serve the larger strategic thesis rather than reading as a feature list.

## Imitation prompt
Write an annual update that ties product progress, customer proof, and market change into one infrastructure narrative.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Listing launches without synthesis.
- Using scale adjectives without named proof.
- Sounding like investor relations instead of operating judgment.
