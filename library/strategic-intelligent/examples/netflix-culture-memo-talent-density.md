# Netflix culture memo talent density

- Author: Netflix
- URL: https://jobs.netflix.com/culture

## Why this is good
Netflix’s culture memo became influential because it treats culture as an operating system, not a list of virtues. The writing is blunt about tradeoffs and expectations, which makes the principles usable in hiring, feedback, and management.

## Craft moves
- Defines culture through behaviors and decisions.
- States tradeoffs with unusual directness.
- Makes expectations concrete enough to enforce.
- Avoids bland values language.

## Structure map
1. Core belief.
2. Behavioral expectation.
3. Tradeoff or cost.
4. Management implication.
5. Examples or decision rules.

## Excerpt or summary
Culture memo analysis only; no memo text mirrored.

## Reusable pattern
A culture document should make expectations enforceable and tradeoffs visible.

## Imitation prompt
Write a culture principle that defines the behavior, names the tradeoff, and explains how managers should use it.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Generic values that no one could disagree with.
- Culture claims disconnected from hiring or firing.
- Softening the tradeoff until the principle becomes meaningless.
