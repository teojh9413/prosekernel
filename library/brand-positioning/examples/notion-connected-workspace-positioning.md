# Notion connected workspace positioning

## Source
- Author: Notion
- URL: https://www.notion.so/product

## Why this is good
Notion’s positioning challenge is breadth: docs, wiki, projects, databases, and AI can become mushy if described as features. The stronger move is to frame those pieces as a connected workspace, giving flexibility a container.

## Craft moves
- Gives modularity a single container.
- Frames many use cases as one workspace problem.
- Uses examples to prevent abstraction.
- Lets breadth feel integrated instead of scattered.

## Structure map
1. Unified workspace frame.
2. Core use-case clusters.
3. Examples/templates.
4. Collaboration and scale proof.

## Reusable pattern
When a product is flexible, name the container that makes its flexibility understandable.

## Imitation prompt
Write positioning for a flexible product by naming the unifying container, then showing three concrete use cases.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Listing every feature until the product sounds like mush.
- Saying flexible without showing what flexibility enables.
- Making modularity feel like setup work.
