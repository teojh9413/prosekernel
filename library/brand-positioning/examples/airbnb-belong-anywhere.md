# Airbnb — Belong Anywhere

## Source
- Author: Airbnb / Brian Chesky
- URL: https://news.airbnb.com/biggestnightever/
- Rights note: Metadata and analysis only.

## Why this is good
“Belong anywhere” turns lodging into identity and emotion. It reframes the category from rooms and rentals to a human desire: not feeling like a stranger.

## Craft moves
- Elevates category from transaction to belonging.
- Makes the customer’s emotional state central.
- Creates a worldview that many product features can support.
- Compresses mission into a phrase.

## Structure map
1. Human need.
2. Category reframing.
3. Brand promise.
4. Product/features as proof points.

## Excerpt or summary
Summary: Airbnb positions itself around belonging, not only accommodation.

## Reusable pattern
“We do not merely provide [category function]. We help [person] feel/become [desired identity/state].”

## Imitation prompt
Write a brand positioning note that reframes this product from transaction to human belonging/identity. Keep claims grounded in actual product proof.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Using emotional language the product cannot support.
- Confusing mission with vague inspiration.
