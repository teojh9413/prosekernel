## Why this is good
The 3-2-1 format is strong because it reduces the reader’s decision cost. Subscribers know the shape before opening: compact value, predictable length, and repeatable categories. The format becomes part of the product.

## Craft moves
- Uses a named repeatable structure.
- Keeps sections short enough to finish.
- Turns cadence into trust.
- Makes forwarding easy because the value is legible.

## Structure map
1. Consistent promise.
2. Fixed section counts.
3. Compact units.
4. Repeatable cadence and archive.

## Reusable pattern
For recurring email, make the format itself a promise the reader can remember.

## Imitation prompt
Design a newsletter format with a fixed promise, section counts, cadence, and skim-friendly units.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Changing format every issue.
- Letting the intro consume the value.
- Making subscribers relearn what they get each time.
