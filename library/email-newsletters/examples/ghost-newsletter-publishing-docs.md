## Why this is good
Ghost’s newsletter docs are useful for Humanprint because they show email as a product workflow: audience, members, settings, publishing, and delivery. The writing lesson is to connect editorial promise with operational clarity.

## Craft moves
- Frames newsletter work as a repeatable system.
- Connects audience/member context to publishing choices.
- Keeps setup steps close to editorial outcome.
- Makes delivery implications explicit.

## Structure map
1. Newsletter purpose.
2. Audience/member setup.
3. Writing and publishing path.
4. Delivery and management details.

## Excerpt or summary

## Reusable pattern
For email products, explain the reader promise and the operational workflow together.

## Imitation prompt
Write a newsletter setup guide that connects audience promise, publishing workflow, and delivery choices.

## Anti-patterns to avoid
- Treating newsletters as isolated blasts.
- Ignoring subscriber context.
- Separating editorial purpose from tool steps.
