# Humanprint Writing Scorecard

Score every important draft from 0-100.

## 1. Clarity — 20 points

- 0-5: point is buried or unclear
- 6-10: understandable but slow
- 11-15: clear but could be sharper
- 16-20: point lands fast and cleanly

## 2. Specificity — 20 points

- 0-5: mostly abstractions
- 6-10: some examples but generic claims remain
- 11-15: concrete in most sections
- 16-20: names, numbers, mechanisms, scenes, and details carry the piece

## 3. Proof — 20 points

- 0-5: claims without evidence
- 6-10: weak proof or source-list theater
- 11-15: good evidence, some gaps
- 16-20: proof chain makes the argument hard to ignore

## 4. Voice / Judgment — 15 points

- 0-5: generic AI tone
- 6-10: competent but faceless
- 11-15: distinct choices, point of view, and human judgment

## 5. Structure — 15 points

- 0-5: list-shaped mush
- 6-10: organized but predictable
- 11-15: structure creates momentum and meaning

## 6. Persuasion Fit — 10 points

- 0-3: wrong awareness stage or unclear ask
- 4-7: decent fit
- 8-10: message matches reader state and desired action

## Automatic penalties

Subtract:

- 15 for fake citations or unverifiable proof
- 10 for legacy inflation without evidence
- 10 for generic AI phrases
- 10 for padded “future prospects” conclusion
- 5 for platform-mismatched formatting

## Passing bar

- 80+ publishable
- 70-79 revise
- under 70 rewrite from structure
