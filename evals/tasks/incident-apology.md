# Eval Task — Incident Apology

## Task
Write an incident apology for a product outage that affected customers for 47 minutes.

## Reader
Current customers who want accountability, not brand voice.

## Required pattern coverage
- `PATTERN_CRISIS_001`
- `PATTERN_EXPLAIN_001`

## Must demonstrate
- Impact, timeline, cause, remediation, and prevention.
- Plain ownership without defensive framing.
- Different levels of detail for busy readers and technical readers.

## Failure modes
- "We apologize for any inconvenience" as the main substance.
- No concrete timeline or prevention step.
- Over-explaining engineering without naming customer impact.
