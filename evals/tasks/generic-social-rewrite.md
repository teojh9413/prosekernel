# Eval Task — Generic Social Post Rewrite

## Task
Rewrite a generic LinkedIn/X post about AI writing into a sharper post that teaches one portable idea.

## Reader
Builders and writers who skim fast and reward compressed insight.

## Required pattern coverage
- `PATTERN_HOOK_001`
- `PATTERN_PROOF_001`

## Must demonstrate
- A named distinction or tension in the first two lines.
- Specific mechanism or example instead of generic AI commentary.
- Short, non-formulaic structure suitable for social reading.

## Failure modes
- Thread-template cadence with no idea.
- Broad claims about "the future of AI".
- Ending with a vague engagement question.
