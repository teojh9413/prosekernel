# Strong social rewrite fixture

Bad AI writing has a tell: it raises the temperature while lowering the evidence.

Humanprint fixes the order. First retrieve examples. Then choose a pattern. Then draft. Then ask the ugly question: if we remove the product name, could this post describe anything?

Rule: do not make the claim bigger. Make the proof harder to ignore, because the reader trusts the example before the adjective.

Example: instead of a vague productivity promise, write "the draft named 6 features and zero reader objections; the rewrite cuts 4 features and answers the objection in line one."
