# Strong launch email fixture

Subject: Stop publishing drafts that sound like the same assistant wrote them

Most AI drafts fail in the same place: the sentence is clean, but the judgment is missing. Humanprint gives agents a checklist before they write: retrieve 3 relevant examples, pick the craft pattern, draft against the reader's moment, then score the result for proof and genericness.

In the seed corpus, 80 annotated examples now map to 12 reusable pattern families. That means a launch email can borrow structure from lifecycle copy, proof from strategic memos, and compression from social posts without copying anyone's words.

Use it when a draft matters enough to deserve taste. Start with `humanprint search-examples "write a launch email"`, then run `humanprint scorecard draft.md` before publishing.
