# Opening Patterns

## Point-first

Lead with the conclusion, then support it.

Use for memos, strategic writing, executive updates.

## Tension-first

Begin with a contradiction or unresolved problem.

Use for essays, social writing, persuasive writing.

## Scene-first

Begin with a concrete moment.

Use for narrative nonfiction, speeches, founder stories.

## Proof-first

Begin with surprising evidence.

Use when data is the strongest hook.

## Avoid

- throat-clearing
- universal claims
- history-of-the-world openings
- “this article will...”
