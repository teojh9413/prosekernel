# Transition Patterns

Good transitions show the reader why the next idea follows.

## Useful transitions

- That sounds small until you see the mechanism.
- The pattern gets clearer in the second example.
- This is where most teams make the wrong tradeoff.
- The objection is fair. It is also incomplete.
- The important distinction is not X vs Y. It is A vs B.

## Bad transitions

- Furthermore
- Moreover
- Additionally
- It is important to note
- In conclusion

Use plain logic instead of academic glue.
